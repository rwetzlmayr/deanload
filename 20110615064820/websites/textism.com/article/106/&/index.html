Att: Entertainment Weekly · 6 April 01

I hear some writer named David Eggs is in town – likely within blocks of where I write this – a circumstance worthy of a cover story in our local alternaweekly. It is not known if the court jester is in tow.

For the tantalization of the corporate media, tonight’s reading will be held at an amusingly, nay, defiantly indie-cred location, which will be filled with reedy women wearing glasses and sensitive but rough-edged men.
