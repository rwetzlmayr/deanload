Sleep Nation · 7 April 01

Last Friday I was walking home from school along the seawall, occasionally passing rows of parallel parked cars. In something like one out of three cars, there was somebody having a nap behind the wheel. Often it was courier or parts-delivery vehicle or something, but just as often it was bland looking people in business gear in Taurii and Sentras, heads leaned back, eyes closed, mouths open.

I figured all these groggy people being on the same stretch of road having naps was a coincidence, but today I took the same route, and there they were again. Real estate agents, computing consultants, guys in vans with mullets. Asleep behind the wheel. One woman had even gone full recline, curled in the foetal position in a business suit.

There’s something going on, I know it.
