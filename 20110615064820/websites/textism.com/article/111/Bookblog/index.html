Bookblog · 7 April 2001

Noted punch-puller Joe Clark launches another weblog, chronicling the writing of his book Building Accessible Websites, in the production of which I may or may not be involved.

Compared to the endless yammering about usability and user experience of the past few years – in the main a lot of conspicuous noise intended to catch the eye of industry titans and conference organizers – accessibility is a far more interesting issue, one almost entirely ignored by the commercial web design world. How many blind people do you imagine are using the web every day? How many dyslexics? It’s huge.
