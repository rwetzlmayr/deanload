WebWriting Scripts · 23 April 01

The WebWriting Applescripts for Tex-Edit Plus have been overhauled and tweaked and chucked under the chin and called incorrigible. They await your download.

The Preflight Cruncher is much smarter about converting dubious characters like é, ™, ¢, ü, and ¥ to HTML entities, is marginally smarter about dealing with blockquotes, and is superduper smarter about block formatting.

Newly added to the suite is a frightfully sophisticated previewing script that will load current text in IE LINKED TO A CASCADING STYLE SHEET (!), so checking how things will look online is just that much more, um, robust.

Enjoy in good health.
