Barbarians :: 6 MAY 01

Two of the most generous and gracious people I’ve ever met (she’s kind and beautiful, he’s a massive, ursine Scot) live about 90 minutes outside of Vancouver in a place called Mission. Their house is packed tight with books, prints, music, stuff; they have a goddam Jack Shadbolt over the kitchen table. Next to the house is a barn full of hand-operated letterpresses and cold metal type and bookbinding gear, in which they publish (hand-typeset, print and bind) obscenely handsome limited-edition books.

If you like, you can visit them and take workshops on bookmaking, or, like me, you can just stew there coveting their life, fantasizing about chucking your computer into a river.
