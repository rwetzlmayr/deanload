Crystalline · 11 May 2001

Andy at Lines & Splines (whose helpful error-checking of Twenty Faces I should cease failing to acknowledge), posted a drawing of lilliputian typo-thug Beatrice Warde by Eric Gill (diary entry, 1929: “To BW in eve – sup with her. SM [Stanley Morison] came later and I drew his hands and drew BW after. Slept with BW and she with me ... but did not commit adultery – we really slept”).

Looking at the drawing through a painkilled fog, I heard myself say, “nice goblets.”
