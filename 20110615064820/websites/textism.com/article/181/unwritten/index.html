Unwritten · 13 May 01

Designing a book jacket frequently involves an attempt to cook down amorphous themes to a single visual gesture; it can be tricky work, especially with fiction. Finding an image that sets the emotional temperature of the text without clogging it with other meaning can mean days of research.

Sometimes I come across images which demand to grace books that don’t exist: sometimes it’s great, goofy, Borgesian fun to come up with the titles of these books.

Want to have a go? The best suggestion will be incorporated into a book jacket design, with the winning contributor’s name as author.
