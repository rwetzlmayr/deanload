Verbatim · 31 May 2001

The only out-and-out fan letter I’ve ever sent (it was late and I was a little drunk; that needn’t detract from the sentiment):

Date: Sat, 21 Apr 2001 01:02:18 -0700
Subject: Happy Friday
From: Dean Allen
To: Jeffrey Zeldman

Zeldman you fucker:
  • You write well, with clarity and economy
  • You care about important things
  • You’re generous in an arena racked with cynicism
  • You get that design is a craft, not a satisfier of egos
  • And that hard work means more than talk
  • You are funny and clever
How do you live with yourself?

- dca

Felicitations and Happy Anniversary, Jeffrey.
