Frequently Asked · 5 June 01

Now and again people ask, hey you smug fuck, what’s the deal with cardigans? You wear a lot of sweaters or some damn thing, Joe?

The answer to the question is no. I find them unflattering on my already doughy physique.

The name derives from a tendency to view books as living, breathing entities; indeed the terminology of page design is vaguely anatomical: running heads, shoulders, feet. Hell, a quantity of text is called a body. It follows that book jackets and covers may be thought of as a sort of garment; one that serves the primary role of advertising what lies inside, hence more ornamental in value than practical. And these ornamental garments of advertising open up. Like a cardigan.
