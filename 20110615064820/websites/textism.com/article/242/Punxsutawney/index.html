Punxsutawney · 12 June 2001

My own private Groundhog Day.

Each time I switch on the television there’s this dopey ad, a gormless retro fantasy piece in which the promise of dot com riches suddenly, miraculously isn’t a stupid, toxic joke designed to transfer your money into the pockets of asswipes named Gates, Ellison and Roth. The ad raises two questions:

  1. What must the actors be thinking, instructed as they are to wig out and shriek in front of a fucking computer monitor? As if any cubicle jockey has ever done this.
  2. Precisely who is the target demo here? Customers don’t care. Small businesses surely wince at this smug replay of hype and lies. Is it CEOs? Will a single twit executive put down the remote control, pick up his Blackberry, and jot down “switch to UPS ASAP?

Might as well pitch Boo as the next big thing.
