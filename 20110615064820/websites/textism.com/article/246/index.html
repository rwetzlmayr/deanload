Buddy Coleridge :: 14 JUNE 01

Even those unfamiliar with the alarmingly tiny swirl of incest, narcissism and self-loathing that is Canadian Literature won’t be shocked to learn that the same generational resentments and wars between factions present in every other literary community in the world also churn away here. And, just as elswehere, all the border skirmishes and declarations nailed to church doors have sweet fuck-all to do with qualities of writing or ideas.

But I’m as bored with Margaret Atwood as the next person, so hearing that former Kid in the Hall Scott Thompson so scandalized the CanLit mafia gathered for the Griffin Poetry Prize the other night – musing about body fluids, wagging a dildo in Anne Michaels’ face – that the Pegster, a proud ’60s radical and former President of PEN Canada, had to leave her seat, well, I’ll admit to having had a brief, but polite, chuckle.
