Memory:: 24 JUNE 01

O how I long for a simpler time: when America’s teens showed a clean divide between Nirvana kids and Guns ’n Roses kids; when Walter Miller defined personal narrative on the web. A time before the arrival of that which stole our hope and innocence: leaping Paddys, filmmaking “painters”, the modern urban columnist, and the departure of Tesh from ET.
