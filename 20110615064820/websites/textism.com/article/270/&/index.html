Fun with Macintoshes · 25 June 01

If you use keychains to store passwords in OS 9.x, you’ll know the annoyance of having to enter master passwords every time you restart. Applescript is here to help. If you paste this into the Script Editor, change the relevant bits, save as a run-only applet, and put it somewhere useful like the Startup Items folder (if you’re not on a shared Mac), you will find this shockingly convenient.
