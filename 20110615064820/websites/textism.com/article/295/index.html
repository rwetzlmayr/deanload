Redmond Logic:: 18 JULY 01

Gosh, it’s just super-duper galling, seeing animatronic machines like Bill Gates, Dubya and the RIAA get away with murder because of the tenacity and ambition of lawyers. Now that Microsoft is above the law and getting snippy about it, one feels nervous; one gets testy.

Three times in recent weeks I’ve launched PowerPoint with the express intention of producing something that demonstrates the fundamental design flaws of PowerPoint – the built-in hostility to clear language, the loopy logic of the seven-point slide, the sheer hideousness of the output – and each time the sonofabitch has crashed on me. Three for three. Spooky.
