To the Malls :: 7 MARCH 01

Not exactly a paid-up member of the alliance of ambient/trance/lite-goth/chill/vacuum cleaner fan clubs, in the main preferring music with a smidgen of vitality to it. Nonetheless I’m addicted to this Delerium song, though I can’t tell you why. Pretty sure the appearance of the Mediæval Bæbes on the chorus isn’t it.

If it were the 1960s, and I was the age I am now, it would perhaps be at this stage that I’d catch myself snapping fingers along to the Muzak in Sears.
