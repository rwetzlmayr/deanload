Aimless - 28 July 01

I can’t get past this. I’m fucked up by it. Ford tries admirably, managing to turn it into a new resolve, channelling anger into purpose; but I can’t do anything with it – this rhino at the dinner table the past week. All I know for certain:

—No one can claim anything from Carlo Giuliani’s death; any lessons learned were too expensive.

—The reactions of corporate apologists have been utterly shameful.

—Diane Francis should shut her fucking cakehole.
