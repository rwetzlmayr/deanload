Fug · 11 August 2001

And now, for no particular reason, three two fun facts about Norman Mailer and one about Gore Vidal:*

  1. Squaring off against Mailer Gore Vidal in a 1968 debate, William F. Buckley Jr. said, “Now listen, you queer! Stop calling me a pro-crypto Nazi or I’ll sock you in your goddamn face and you’ll stay plastered.”
  2. His name is one that improves greatly when spoken as a spoonerism.
  3. When The Naked and the Dead was published in 1948, much of the language was deemed too coarse, and Mailer had to have his characters use the term fug. When Dorothy Parker subsequently met him at a party, she said, “So you’re the young man who can’t spell fuck?”
* Some stickler for ‘accuracy’ wrote to correct this assertion.
