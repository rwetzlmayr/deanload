Four Things · 6 September 01

  • Everyone smokes, everywhere, all the time; infants consume mild, two-puff training cigarettes in the cradle.
  • Everyone drives at maximum speed, all the time, and the roadsigns engage you directly: You do not have right of way; You are in an urban area, slow down.
  • Flea markets show that crap, even if French, is still crap.
  • All people over 60 have heroic tales of La Résistance; rather like boomers and Woodstock.
