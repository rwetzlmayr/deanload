Sort of New · 29 October 01

Not new per se but newly added to the column to my right (your left) are some AppleScripts that I made available awhile ago, which kind of went missing for a bit, and for which requests have been many. The keychain one is handy if you hate entering your master password each time your computer starts up because the fucking thing just crashed or the cat shut the thing down after a marathon session of Leisure Suit Larry or some such. (Say, you aren’t still shutting your Mac down at the end of the day, are you? Really, there’s no need. Just put it to Sleep.)

The one for double arrows at both ends of window scrollbars seems like no big thing at first glance, however once in use, like regular sex or television, it is very nearly impossible to live without.

Minor improvements also to the Word HTML Cleaner, which, for a birrovalaff, now shows what the Microsoft code looked like before it was cleaned up. Hoo!
