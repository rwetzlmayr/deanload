Pax · 25 December 01

It’s just gone Christmas here, this house standing quiet in the dark, crackling snow lingering outside; beside me Herself folded into a lovely warm curl of sleep while the television rattles on; Oliver over there, one eye open, calculating that tomorrow it’ll be lamb kidneys instead of kibble.

I want only sleep. And none of this to end, ever.
