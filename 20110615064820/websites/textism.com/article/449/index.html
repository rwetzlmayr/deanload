Go Shaggy, Go Scooby · 4 February 2002

After a period of herculean idleness on my part, the Web Writing AppleScripts for Tex-Edit Plus have had a retooling, and now work better, faster, and in an improved fashion.

What they are in brief: scripts to aid in the production of text for the web, that run within a dandy shareware text editor for the Macintosh (MacOS 9.x or OSX). The scripts will, for example, make a hyperlink on the fly to whatever page is loaded in your browser, format styled text in a nice standards-compliant way, ensure that dodgy characters such as “°” and “¶” and “Å” are going to show up as intended on a web page, and generally add to one’s quality of life.

Use as you will, in good health.
