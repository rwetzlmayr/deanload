Me Shop Pretty One Day · 31 March 02

—If you please?
—Yes, sir.
—I search for fabric, but with holes.
—Fabric with holes.
—Yes, fabric to see well the light through.
—Light?
—Yes, with good certainty, on the windows, for the not-good travelling in of small insects, for example flies and mosquitos. I can’t know exact the word at this instant.
—Ah. Mosquito net.
—It is that!
—Do you want premade frames, or just the net?
—No, because I will do the manufacture of the frames with my hands, these here.
—Outside.
—Outside?
—It is outside.
—Oh, just at the side of the building supplies – I understand. Thank you.
—No, it is me.
