It Was Not · 9 July 02

Self portrait at 6:10 PM, 9 July 2002:

  1. Coming home, driving fast across the long straightaway that lays between Pompignan and the hills to the south.

  2. Vineyards whizzing by: the space between rows forms a tapered line to the horizon a dozen times per second.

  3. The stereo, ‘You Shook Me All Night Long’ by AC/DC, is painfully loud.

  4. I am hungry.

  5. Morosely cataloguing 1,575 unfinished things, and all ensuing guilt.

  6. Wondering whether it’s more sensible to push a particular SQL result to a multidimensional associative array or to loop through the resulting array as it stands.

  7. Was that a rabbit?
