Syndication · 25 July 2002

A small change to this site’s RSS feed: subscribers previously received the three most current postings in their entirety; as that seemed a bit pushy, I’ve changed the feed simply to include the title and a brief description of the ten most current postings. This seems to me an improvement, however complaints and suggestions may be directed through the usual channels.

Incidentally, if you’re using OS X you’ll want to download NetNewsWire, a slick new Cocoa news gatherer; it’s by far the best RSS reader yet.
