Capital · 21 September 2002

Three random things:

  • Women and, er, caskets.
  • ‘My body is simply shells for my donkey’.
  • ‘A new bread of secret agent’.

Two good causes:

  • The tireless Mark Woods of wood s lot is suddenly without a computer; a dilemma, one could speculate, similar to Gary Busey suddenly having no access to decent blow, or perhaps Ted Nugent – face to face with a rutting moose – finding no arrows in his quiver. Euan Semple is collecting donations to help Mark acquire a new rig. wood s lot has provided a great repository of readable things for years. It’s been worth ten of your bucks, surely.
  • Toke of K10k is in a similar boat [not any more]. Be generous to the generous.

One decent offer:

  • Fellow dialup subscribers in France who find themselves weary of propping up an odious monopoly posting €80 billion losses may not yet know that Tiscali is offering flat rate 24/7 dialup connections for cheap.
