O Vancouverites · 20 March 2001

Note that this Thursday the 22nd, from 7–9 pm, there will be a panel discussion held at SFU Harbour Centre called “E-Books: Transforming the Way We Read, the Way We Publish.” On the panel will be Julie Blackwell, product manager for Microsoft Reader, as well as Tiro type designer and OpenType enthusiast John Hudson, who will have opinions.

Should be good. I regret that I won’t be there: I’ll be down the hall acting like I know what I’m talking about.
