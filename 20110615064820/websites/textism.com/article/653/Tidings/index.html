Tidings · 24 December 02

As they say, ‘all the best’ to you and yours, celebrate as you will, be a good person, love the one(s) you’re with, do something nice like give money to good causes, quit advancing political agenda on the backs of dead strangers, stop being so worried all the time, remember Joe Strummer’s raspy cry in ‘Death or Glory’, and know again the singular joy brought about by the insertion of turkey, stuffing, cranberry and gravy between two slices of bread.

Merry Christmas.
