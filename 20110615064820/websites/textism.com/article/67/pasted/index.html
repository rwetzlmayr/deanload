Pasted · 22 March 01

Photos of doe-eyed, anorexic gamines apparently about to engage in three-way sex with two gay models. A hammering devotion to fifteen minutes of midcentury design. Serious thinking about “consumer culture.” You gots to love Wallpaper. Once I saw Tyler Brûlée, with a completely straight face, inform an incredulous Evan Seeyouinseven that “life is lived at 36,000 feet,” (I needed a new TV anyway, all hail Elvis).

So I keep asking myself if these people actually exist – these rich, thin, silent people without bodily functions – I’ve never seen one. I’ve concluded that, like black holes, you can’t see them so much as survey their effect. And by that of course I mean shopping. Oh, whee, shop for junk, shop for functionless garb, shop for porn.
