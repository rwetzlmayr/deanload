Gesture · 3 September 03

So I placed an order for some dog training equipment. Sent the credit card digits, got the confirmation email, assumed the order was on its way. Four days later, this arrived:

WHY LEERBURG WILL NOT SELL TO FRANCE
In the late 1980’s and early 1990’s I would not accept any orders from South Africa. I refused to accept orders from this country until a partied was dismantled. In the grand scheme of things this did not affect anything. But it made me feel good.
Thanks
Caya
leerburg.com

And, you know, I haven’t stopped laughing since.

You go, Caya!
