Energetic · 29 March 2001

Giant of Journalism™ John Fraser offers groggy advice to the new editor of Maclean’s:

“[H]ire some incredibly young people and tap into their energy and outrageousness.”

He must be referring to the ever-vital breath of youth at the Post.

How young is “incredibly young” anyway? Screaming toddlers flinging feces and outrageousness, running around the office, pitching story ideas? Shambling mooks in do-rags and Eminem gear looming everywhere going, “let’s do this”? Mallrats from Etobicoke who respond to every single stimulus, no matter how slight, with “oh my god!”?
