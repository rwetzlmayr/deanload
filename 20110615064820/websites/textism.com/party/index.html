Designer-writer-physicist Dean Allen, 35, publisher of Cardigan Industries and Textism, designer of much earnest midlist, once known for tireless innovation and media smarts – though now mostly just kind of pleasant to have around – is moving to France. The reasons for this are myriad and quite good, actually, as far as reasons to move to France go.

An event has been planned to mark this departure (and catch up on this and that), at which your presence will be most welcome.
