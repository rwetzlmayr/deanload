Fournier

Pierre Simon Fournier
1742

Another text face which was sublime in metal, and just sort of okay in digital. The digital version shown here tends to lose its character at small sizes, so use it big or not at all.

As Jenson breathes the Venetian Renaissance, Fournier is the embodiment of French neoclassicism.
