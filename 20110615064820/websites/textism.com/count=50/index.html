Verisign, Again - 2 May 03

How pleasing to learn that the Attorney General of the State of New York is investigating the impact of the business practices of Verisign on residents of the state.

Mike Wasylik, who did pro bono legal work on behalf of the great Leslie Harpold when her domain hoopla.com was stolen, has been asked by the NY Attorney General’s office to gather and provide information from those whose lives have gained new dimension through ‘The Value of Trust™’.

If you have any, please pass it on.

TextBox - 24 April 03

So this is what’s been chewing up the time.

There are a number of options in the works for making available paid website hosting integrated with Textpattern (this site’s inchoate but durn near ready content management system), but TextBox is the hosting service that’s basically ready to go, is free, and is run by, er, me.

No domain registration is required: sites are reached at yoursite.textbox.org.

Writers interested in publishing a site with Textpattern pre-installed, in a tailor-made hosting environment, with design assistance (within reason) from the putz who wrote it, might find this of interest.

For now, twenty are available. That’s it for now. Thanks to all who wrote.

More will be accepted soon, after the pending beta release of Textpattern: The Growth Years.
